We stand up for your goals & successes!

The first "PowerPulse Fitness" was opened in 2023, and we now have several locations. We have also planned further locations for the future: Enjoy Fitness Clubs stand for two different studio concepts... Simply "Fit & Well".

We attach great importance to top devices of well-known manufacturers, a varied offer, qualified staff, the best possible care, and a well-kept and familiar ambience and all at a fair price.

We adapt our opening hours to your everyday life, and all Enjoy Fitness Clubs are open 24 hours a day! Our team already includes more than 35 employees in the areas of management, club managers, fitness trainers, instructors, office and service staff, trainees, caretakers, and cleaners. We are a big family and very proud of every single one.
